How I Will Teach Tumper

This site is to demonstrate a different type of pattern, one that we already use in note taking and gardens but just approached in a different way. Here I lay out a manual I plan to write for Tumper. Tumper is me in a future life that has no knowledge of what I know now. This is a work in progress but some of it is solidified and I will try my best to point out how strongly I feel about certain items, though I suspect Tumper will know by my tone of voice.

With this pattern, we pretend that reincarnation exists and that we are compiling a repo for our future selves. This particular one is very specific. It is about using ancient esoteric technologies in the modern world. In short, it is the search for innate bliss combined with wisdom compassion, the tools of the trade to accomplish this, and what works for me so Tumper can use it as a trusty guide. It is a bootstrapping manual in the event there are no gurus readily available for Tumper, but hopefully still an internet. Feel free to browse.
